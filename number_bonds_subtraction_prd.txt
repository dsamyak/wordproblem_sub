1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This document defines product requirements for the "Number Bonds for Subtraction" interactive lesson module, delivered as Lesson 3.2 within Intellia SG's Grade 1 Math program. The module targets Singapore Primary 1 students aged 6–7 and teaches the use of number bond diagrams to understand and solve subtraction within 20, fully aligned with the Singapore MOE Mathematics Framework and the Primary 1 Targeting Mathematics (1A/1B) syllabus.

The product is a standalone web page hosted at:
https://intelliasg.com/courses/grade-1-math/lessons/number-bonds-for-subtraction/

It is built using React (Vite + JSX, JavaScript/CSS) and designed to strictly mirror the visual and UX structure established at https://readingnumbers.vercel.app/ and the repository https://github.com/dsamyak/readingnumbers.

The module follows Intellia's 5-phase learner journey:
  Phase 1 — WONDER   → curiosity hook
  Phase 2 — STORY    → narrative-based concept introduction
  Phase 3 — SIMULATE → sandbox-style interactive simulation
  Phase 4 — PLAY     → IntelliPlay™ gamified practice
  Phase 5 — REFLECT  → journal / LearnFlow AI prompt

6. PHASE 3 — SIMULATION DESIGN (Detail)

6.1 Station A — Counter Take-Away (Concrete)
  Visual: A row of colourful circular counters (max 20)
  Interaction:
    - Drag counters into a "taken away" bin (animated disappear)
    - OR tap counters to cross them out
  Display: Number bond diagram updates in real-time
    → Whole: [9]   Part taken: [4]   Part left: [?]
  Teaching: Child discovers the "part left" by doing, not telling

6.2 Station B — Number Bond Builder (Pictorial)
  Visual: Animated number bond diagram (circle-branch-circle)
  Interaction:
    - Whole is shown at top
    - One part is given
    - Child drags the correct number into the empty part circle
    - Counters animate to fill the circle
  Variants:
    - Find the missing part (whole – part = ?)
    - Find the whole (? = part + part — reinforces addition link)
  Number range: 0–10 in Station B, 0–20 in Station B+

6.3 Station C — Number Sentence (Abstract)
  Visual: Number sentence with one blank: [9] – [4] = [___]
  Interaction:
    - Number pad (large tap-friendly buttons 0–9/0–20)
    - OR drag-and-drop number tiles
  Scaffolding: Number bond diagram shown as hint if needed
  Teaching Point: The abstract sentence is just the bond in a line

7. PHASE 4 — QUESTION BANK (100 Randomised Questions)

7.1 Question Types (10 types × 10 questions = 100)

Type | Description                          | Example
-----|--------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------
Q1   | Number bond diagram — find part      | [8] – [3] = [?]  (bond diagram shown)
Q2   | Subtraction sentence — fill blank    | 7 – ___ = 4
Q3   | Picture take-away — count remaining  | 9 stars, 5 crossed → How many left?
Q4   | Word problem — Singapore context     | Mia has 12 sweets. Gives 5 to Jun. Left?
Q5   | Fact family — find missing sentence  | 6+4=10, 4+6=10, 10–4=? → complete the family
Q6   | True/False — is the bond correct?    | Is 8 – 3 = 4? True or False?
Q7   | Part-whole — which number bond fits? | Show 4 number bonds → which matches 10–7=3?
Q8   | Number before/after link             | ___ – 2 = 5 (what is the whole?)
Q9   | Double subtraction                   | 15 – 6 = ___ (within 20)
Q10  | Reverse: find the whole              | ___ – 4 = 6 (what is the whole?)

8. GAMIFICATION DESIGN

8.1 Reward System
  Stars (⭐):   Earned per 10-question set (1–3 stars based on score)
  XP Points:   10 XP correct first try | 7 XP second try | 5 XP with hint
  Streak 🔥:  Fire counter for consecutive correct answers
  Streak bonus: +5 XP per correct answer when streak ≥ 5

8.2 Badges (Unlockable)
  🏅 "Bond Beginner"    — Complete Wonder + Story phases
  🥈 "Subtraction Star" — Complete Simulate phase (all 3 stations)
  🥇 "Bond Master"      — Score 80%+ on Play phase (Challenge)
  💎 "Perfect Bond"     — 100% on any 10-question world
  🔥 "Streak Legend"    — Achieve a streak of 10+
  🌟 "Full Journey"     — Complete all 5 phases (lesson complete)

8.3 Feedback Mechanics
  ✅ Correct:   Bounce animation + sound + "Amazing! You cracked the bond! 🎉"
  ❌ Incorrect: Gentle shake + "Let's try again! Look at the number bond 🔢"
  Hint 1:      Number bond diagram highlighted
  Hint 2:      Counter animation shows take-away visually
  After 2 fails: Answer revealed with animated explanation
  No negative scoring — encouragement-first, always.

8.4 World Map (IntelliPlay™ Level Progression)
  World 1 — "Candy Land"    (Questions 1–10,  within 10, easy)
  World 2 — "Jungle Trek"   (Questions 11–20, within 10, medium)
  World 3 — "Ocean Deep"    (Questions 21–30, within 15, easy-med)
  World 4 — "Sky Islands"   (Questions 31–40, within 15, medium)
  World 5 — "Volcano Peak"  (Questions 41–50, within 15, hard)
  World 6 — "Space Station" (Questions 51–60, within 20, easy)
  World 7 — "Dragon Cave"   (Questions 61–70, within 20, medium)
  World 8 — "Crystal Tower" (Questions 71–80, within 20, medium)
  World 9 — "Rainbow Bridge"(Questions 81–90, within 20, hard)
  World 10— "Number Palace"  (Questions 91–100, mixed, hard)

  Each world unlocks only when ≥6/10 correct (1-star minimum)
  3 stars required for bonus "hidden challenge" in each world

8.5 Mascot (LearnFlow AI Companion)
  Character: Friendly robot (matching Intellia branding — "LearnFlow")
  Mood states: idle | curious | happy | thinking | celebrating
  Appears in: Wonder hook, Story narration, Reflect phase
  Reacts to: Correct answer, badge unlock, streak milestone

10. CONTENT REQUIREMENTS

10.1 Simulation Visuals
  Number bond diagrams: SVG-rendered, animated fill/empty
  Counters: Circular emoji-style items (stars, apples, cookies)
  Part-whole model: Large, clear circles and connecting lines
  Bar model reference: Simple visual introduction (not required to solve)

10.2 Question Bank Coverage
  All 10 question types × 10 questions = 100 unique questions
  Questions randomised per session (Fisher-Yates shuffle)
  No two sessions present the same question order
  MCQ distractors are plausible (within ±3 of correct answer)

10.3 Word Problems — Singapore MOE Style
  Format: "Wei Ming had ___ [objects]. He gave/used/lost ___ [objects].
           How many did he have left?"
  Objects: cookies, stickers, marbles, balloons, books, fish, apples
  Names:   Singapore-context (see §7.4)
  Aligned to "Let's Think Along" style from Targeting Mathematics 1A

10.4 Audio
  Number narration (0–20) via Web Speech API
  Question read-aloud for every question
  Celebration sounds on correct/badge/streak
  Background music (ambient, toggleable, off by default)
